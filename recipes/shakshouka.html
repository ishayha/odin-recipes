Shakshouka Recipes


Shaksouka is an affordable and quick meal, fit perfectly for a broke colleges students


ingredients :

  • Paprika
  • Raw or canned tomato
  • Spices
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Eggs
  • Smoked paprika
  • Cilantro Leaves
  • feta cheese

Steps

  1. diced paprika, onions and garlic
  2. sauteed onions and paprika till soft and transluscent, then add garlic
  3. after that, put in a desired amount of smoked paprika and spices
  4. if you use raw tomato, you can char them first, peel and diced, then put together with the rest. if canned just cut them and pour all in
  5. cooked until boiling and reduce the heat to simmer. if you have a blender, you can blended everything till fine
  6. create a hole in the middle of the sauce and creacked egg there
  7. cover the lid until egg cooked
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves and feta cheese
  9. Shakshouka is ready
